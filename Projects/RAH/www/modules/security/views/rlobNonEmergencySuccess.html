What should I expect after booking?
Immediately following completion of booking, you will receive a confirmation email outlining your booking details. This signifies your booking has been confirmed.
Whilst a REDIMED team member may contact you to confirm your booking details or provide advice relating to your appointment, you should proceed as normal with the appointment.
REDIMED advises its patients to phone the relevant clinic prior to arrival to ensure the practitioner is running on time as per their schedule.
What happens if I cancel my appointment?
If you cannot attend your scheduled appointment, please notify the relevant clinic at your earliest convenience. You may be required to pay a cancellation fee depending on how late the clinic is advised of your intent to cancel.
