Consent

The work fitness assessment is a test of your capacity to undertake work postures and work activities. The assessment will take into consideration the functional capacity required to perform the job you are applying for. You will be expected to set you r own limits and will not be asked to undertake any task that you feel you can not safely perform. The evaluator will cease the assessment if in their opinion they feel you are unsafe to proceed. Each task will be fully explained to you before testing
